## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Define America's security posture, alliance structures, and strategic approach to adversaries and global challenges through Integrated Defense Partnerships (IDPs) that distribute risk and build collective capacity.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we maintain alliance commitments while reducing single-point-of-failure risk?
- What industrial and supply chain architecture supports sustained deterrence?
- How do we manage gray-zone conflict without ad hoc crisis deliberation?

**Success Criteria:**
- Clear three-tier partnership framework (IDP/Backstop/Aligned)
- Alliance-wide industrial capacity for surge production
- Automatic response curves for gray-zone activity
- Resilient supply chains with N+2 redundancy
