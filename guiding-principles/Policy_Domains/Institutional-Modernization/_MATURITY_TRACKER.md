## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Modernize democratic institutions through reusable execution instruments that translate democratically authorized objectives into operational reality under bounded authority, cost, and time constraints.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we enable adaptive governance without technocracy?
- What execution structures solve delivery failure without expanding state power?
- How do we deploy public capital deliberately and reversibly?

**Success Criteria:**
- Execution Corps instrument specification deployed across domains
- Public Capital Authority operational with tax-expenditure conversion underway
- Government capable of pilot-first implementation with planned exit
