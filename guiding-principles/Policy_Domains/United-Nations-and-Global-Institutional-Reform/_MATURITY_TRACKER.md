**Purpose:**
This folder is the physical location for files whose domain YAML is set to either `National_Security` (subdomain: UN_Reform) or `United-Nations-and-Global-Institutional-Reform`. The UN reform architecture is tracked primarily in the **National_Security domain tracker** under the UN_Reform subdomain. ## Notes & Context

- **Primary tracker is National_Security** — all substantive gap analysis and phase tracking for these files lives there
- The UN reform architecture avoids both US withdrawal from multilateral institutions (ceding legitimacy) and naive multilateralism (pretending the current SC veto structure is functional) — RSCs as distributed legitimacy mechanisms; enforcement ladder as operational capability
- Platform emergency directives is a distinctive instrument — digital crisis governance at the international level (coordinated deceptive practices at state-sponsored scale; cross-border infrastructure attacks) without requiring SC consensus; complements the CDE enforcement framework in Technology_and_Data
- If this becomes a standalone domain, the Trade_Policy/WTO reform and National_Security/UN reform should be treated as a unified international institutional reform agenda
