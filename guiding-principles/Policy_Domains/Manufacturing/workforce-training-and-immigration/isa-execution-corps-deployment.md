# Immigration Services and Administration — Execution Corps Deployment Model

## Purpose

This brief defines how ISA operates when embedded within Execution Corps deployments.

ISA maintains its role as **lifecycle administrator and system integrity authority**, while operating closer to the point of labor deployment.

---

## Role in Deployment

ISA acts as the **on-the-ground integrity layer** for labor pathways.

It ensures that:

- workers arrive into functional employment conditions  
- employer commitments are fulfilled  
- compliance is maintained without friction  
- lifecycle transitions are executed cleanly  

---

## Embedded Structure

ISA is deployed as part of a **Corridor Labor Cell**, alongside:

- WCA (coordination)  
- Execution Corps (project delivery)  
- employers and local institutions  

ISA presence is:

- operational, not enforcement-heavy  
- integrated with local Workforce Access Points  
- continuously connected to central systems  

---

## Core Functions in Deployment

### 1. Arrival Verification

ISA confirms:

- worker-job alignment  
- payroll activation  
- wage compliance  
- onboarding readiness  

Entry into the workforce is validated as:

> real and functional, not nominal

---

### 2. Landing Pad Integrity

ISA verifies:

- housing or basic living arrangements  
- access to orientation and services  
- availability of reporting channels  

Failures trigger:

> system-level correction, not worker burden

---

### 3. Continuous Compliance Monitoring

ISA performs:

- passive validation of:
  - employment status  
  - wage adherence  
  - role alignment  

No routine physical inspections occur.

Intervention is:

- triggered  
- targeted  
- rule-based  

---

### 4. Worker Lifecycle Support

Through local access points, ISA provides:

- status clarity  
- renewal pathways  
- transition options  
- issue resolution  

ISA answers:

> “Where am I in the system, and what happens next?”

---

### 5. Employer Compliance Enforcement

ISA ensures:

- payroll transparency  
- adherence to wage standards  
- alignment between reported and actual roles  

Violations result in:

- loss of access to labor pathways  
- enforcement escalation if required  

---

## Interaction with WCA

- WCA creates pathways  
- ISA ensures those pathways are real and maintained  

In deployment:
- coordination is immediate  
- mismatches are resolved quickly  

---

## Enforcement Posture in Deployment

ISA does not operate as a visible enforcement presence.

Instead:

- compliance is system-driven  
- enforcement is triggered by verified violations  
- actions are predictable and auditable  

---

## Constraints

ISA in deployment:

- does not define labor demand  
- does not allocate labor  
- does not conduct routine physical enforcement  

All actions remain:

> system-bound, rule-based, and centrally logged

---

## System Effect

When deployed correctly:

- workers enter stable, verified conditions  
- employer compliance is high  
- enforcement is minimal but effective  
- labor pathways function without friction  

ISA ensures that:

> every authorized job is real, every worker is properly supported, and every pathway is completed lawfully
