## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Create modular, pre-certified public infrastructure standards that enable rapid deployment of transit, utilities, and civic corridors through Special Transit Zones.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we standardize infrastructure without losing regional character?
- What regulatory framework enables fast permitting?
- How do we finance infrastructure spine at scale?

**Success Criteria:**
- Modular spine standards certified and deployable
- Special Transit Zones operational in pilot regions
- Regional architectural pattern books adopted
