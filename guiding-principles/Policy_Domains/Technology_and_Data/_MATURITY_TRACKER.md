**Purpose:**
Modernize government technology, establish digital identity infrastructure, and create governance frameworks for emerging technologies.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we modernize institutions without technocracy?
- What digital identity model balances convenience with privacy?
- How do we govern AI without stifling innovation?

**Success Criteria:**
- Government institutions capable of adaptive, empirical policy
- Digital identity infrastructure deployed
- AI governance framework established
