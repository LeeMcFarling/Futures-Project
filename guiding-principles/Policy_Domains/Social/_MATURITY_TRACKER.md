## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Build the social and cultural infrastructure of civilizational stability — through civic formation pathways, national prestige architecture redesign, and recognition systems that redirect aspiration toward contribution. Address the meaning crisis through institutional design rather than ideology. Create the spatial and social conditions for community formation.

**Key Questions:**
- How does a society redirect aspiration from accumulation to contribution without coercion?
- What institutional infrastructure makes civic contribution culturally visible and competitively rewarding?
- How do national service and civic formation pathways produce the leadership the system's problems require?

**Success Criteria:**
- National Problem Portfolio operational with validated Big Asks and competing teams
- National Contribution Gala established as high-prestige cultural event
- National Capability Tour producing cross-regional civic cohorts
- Civic Acropolis chartered and Registry operational
- Emergent Impact Track recognizing unsolicited breakthrough contributions
