## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Establish a fiscal architecture that treats public capital as strategic investment (not opaque subsidy), rebuilds the capacity for citizens below the dynastic threshold to accumulate generational wealth, and replaces extractive rent patterns above the $50M threshold with a democratically authorized wealth transfer system. The $50M threshold is the organizing spine: below it, the country builds wealth with you; above it, dynasty tax applies.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we deploy public capital with discipline, reversibility, and outcome accountability?
- How does the pre-seed/superannuation architecture rebuild the social contract on wealth accumulation?
- How do we structure dynasty taxation to be credible and litigation-resistant?
- How do we modernize fiscal infrastructure (IRS, tax code) without creating new bureaucratic drag?

**Success Criteria:**
- Public Capital Authority operational with portfolio definitions and recycling mechanics
- Universal Superannuation System replacing Social Security on defined transition timeline
- Dynasty tax and illiquid-asset realization framework deployed with enforcement architecture
- Child Future Accounts (pre-seed) deployed with compound-growth mathematics public and legible
- IRS modernization complete: automated income attestation, fraud reduction, compliance without audit harassment

## Notes & Context

- Domain has **16 files** totaling ~47,265 words — the single largest policy domain by word count
- **Decomposed from Economic_Prosperity** on 2026-05-13 — previously a subdomain, now a standalone domain
- **$50M threshold** is the organizing spine of the entire economic architecture: below it, pre-seed + superannuation + corporate competitiveness; above it, dynasty tax + illiquid asset realization + borrowing-triggered realization; one threshold, two directions, one coherent social contract
- **Pre-seed compound math**: $10,000 at 7% real return from birth ≈ $867,000 at age 67; Social Security pays ~$22,000/year × 20 years = $440,000; pre-seed alone ≈ 2× SS payout at ~2% of the cost — most powerful wealth-building instrument in the stack for non-wealthy families
- **Superannuation Bucket A**: contributions up to the protected cap (indexed to $50M); Bucket B: contributions above cap are taxable capital. Architecture eliminates the exemption that turns retirement accounts into dynasty vehicles.
- **Borrowing-triggered realization** closes the "buy, borrow, die" loophole — the most important enforcement mechanism in the dynasty tax architecture
- 4 files (AGENCY_Public_Capital_Authority.md, Budgetary_Guiding_Principles.md, Capital_Capital_Allocation_Reform.md, Social_Security_Reform.md) are older-format files without version tracking — prose quality varies; three have been superseded by more detailed briefs
- Capital Allocation Reform and Social Security Reform are early drafts (~850–900 words); the detailed specs live in universal-superannuation-system.md and the dynasty stack
- PCA lives in both Budget_and_Fiscal_Policy (financing architecture) and Institutional-Modernization (execution instrument) — the two are complementary; Budget owns the capital structure, Institutional-Modernization owns the deployment mechanism
