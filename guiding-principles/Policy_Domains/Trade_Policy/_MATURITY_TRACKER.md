## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Build a coherent US trade architecture that uses market access as a strategic instrument, activates industrial capacity through tariff governance, creates preferential frameworks with allied partners (IDP/NORAM), and manages adversarial relationships through phase-gated enforcement — without the ad hoc, unpredictable tariff regime that destroys planning horizons for both domestic and allied firms.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we use tariffs as industrial activation instruments without triggering retaliatory spirals?
- What trade architecture deepens IDP alliance cohesion economically?
- How do we manage adversarial trade relationships (China, cartel states) with phase-gated predictability?
- How do we reform the WTO framework without ceding the multilateral rules-based order?

**Success Criteria:**
- Tariff governance framework with transparent, rules-based trigger structure operational
- NORAM manufacturing alignment compact agreed with Canada and Mexico
- IDP trade compact framework providing preferential access tied to integration level
- WTO modernization coalition formed and agenda advanced
- Export controls on critical technologies phase-gated (not blanket) and internationally coordinated

## Notes & Context

- Domain has **8 files** totaling ~10,198 words; all files carry "draft-" prefix reflecting Phase 2–3 development stage
- **Decomposed from Economic_Prosperity** on 2026-05-13 — previously a subdomain with mixed subdomains (Trade_and_Industrial_Policy, Manufacturing, Regional_Industrial_Strategy, Trade_Policy); all rationalized to Trade_Policy
- **Trade architecture is the economic arm of the national security alliance structure** — IDP trade compact and NORAM alignment are economic complements to the IDP military integration framework; the two domains are designed together
- **Tariff governance vs. tariff ad hockery** — the platform's trade architecture is explicitly anti-Trump-tariff-style: all tariff instruments are rules-based, behavior-conditioned, publicly transparent, and subject to WTO notification; predictability is as important as the level
- **China phase gates** in trade (technology, sector-specific) mirror the phase gates in china-strategy-managed-competition-v0.3.md — the two files should be read together; trade is one of the 10 issue-specific tracks in the China managed competition framework
- **Cartel derisking** (cartel-derisking-and-regional-stability-framework.md) is conceptually linked to the Venezuela case study in National_Security/trump-reactions/ — resource sovereignty and odious debt doctrine apply to cartel-captured states as well as direct adversaries
- All 7 "draft-" files are solid Phase 2–3 quality; the "draft-" prefix is a style artifact from when these were exploratory, not an indicator of incomplete thought
