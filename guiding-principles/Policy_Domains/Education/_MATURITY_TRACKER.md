## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Transform K-12 education through a national competency framework, AI-enabled personalization, and principal-led accountability. Build higher education around discovery pre-seeding infrastructure, competency alignment, and labor signal integration. Guarantee outcomes regardless of zip code rather than standardizing inputs.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we guarantee educational outcomes without mandating curriculum?
- What role do teachers play in AI-augmented, mastery-based classrooms?
- How do we connect higher education to labor signal, civic formation, and national problem-solving?
- How do we fund schools equitably without overriding local governance?

**Success Criteria:**
- National Competency Framework operational and adopted across pilot districts
- DES standards body established with trigger protocols active
- AI personalization pilot running in at least one district cohort
- Principal budget autonomy pilots showing measurable outcome improvement
- Higher education linked to competency credentials and labor market signals
