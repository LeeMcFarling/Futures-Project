## Domain Overview

**Purpose:**
Transform K-12 education through AI-enabled personalization, open-source infrastructure, and principal-led accountability while protecting student privacy.

**Key Questions:**
- How do we scale personalized learning without surveillance?
- What role do teachers play in AI-augmented classrooms?
- How do we ensure equity in access to educational technology?

**Success Criteria:**
- Personalized learning pathways available in pilot schools
- Student data protected via SSI and zero-knowledge proofs
- Principal budget autonomy pilots showing results
